# 🏆 KNOCKOUT STAGE GUIDE

## 🎯 **How to Create Knockout Matches (Step by Step)**

### **Step 1: Access Knockout Management**
1. Go to your tournament: `http://127.0.0.1:5001/tournament/1`
2. Click the **"Bracket"** tab
3. Click **"Manage Knockout Stage"** button

### **Step 2: Create Quarter-Final Matches**
1. In the **Quarter Finals** section, click **"Create Match"**
2. Choose **Home Team** from the dropdown (any team you want)
3. Choose **Away Team** from the dropdown (any team you want)
4. Set the **Match Date**
5. Add **Venue** (optional)
6. Click **"Create Match"**

### **Step 3: Update Scores to Advance Teams**
1. After creating matches, click **"Update Score"** on any match
2. Enter the final score (e.g., Home: 2, Away: 1)
3. Click **"Update Score"** - this marks the match as completed
4. The winning team automatically advances to the next stage

### **Step 4: Create Semi-Final Matches**
1. Once ALL quarter-final matches are completed
2. Click **"Create Match"** in the **Semi Finals** section
3. Choose teams from the winners of quarter-finals
4. Repeat the process

### **Step 5: Create Final Match**
1. Once ALL semi-final matches are completed
2. Click **"Create Match"** in the **Final** section
3. Choose the two finalists
4. Update the score to determine the champion!

## 🔧 **Key Features**

✅ **Full Control**: You choose exactly which teams advance
✅ **Flexible Qualification**: No automatic rules - you decide!
✅ **Manual Matchups**: Create any confrontation you want
✅ **Real-time Updates**: Scores update immediately
✅ **Automatic Progression**: Winners automatically advance

## 💡 **Tips**

- **You can choose ANY teams** for knockout, regardless of group position
- **Create matches in order**: Quarter → Semi → Final
- **Update scores** to unlock the next stage
- **Teams automatically advance** when you update scores
- **No limit** on how many teams from each group can advance

## 🚀 **Ready to Start?**

Your tournament is ready for knockout stage! Just follow the steps above and you'll have full control over who plays who in the knockout rounds.
